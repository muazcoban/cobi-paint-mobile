Cobi Paint - Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 2025

Summary: Cobi Paint does NOT collect, store, or share any personal information. The app works completely offline and is safe for children.

1. Introduction

Cobi Paint ("we", "our", or "the app") is a coloring application designed for children. We are committed to protecting the privacy of our users, especially children under the age of 13.

2. Information We Collect

We do not collect any personal information.

  • No names or email addresses
  • No location data
  • No device identifiers
  • No usage analytics
  • No cookies or tracking

3. Data Storage

All data (saved artworks, settings, preferences) is stored locally on your device only. This data never leaves your device and is not transmitted to any servers.

4. Children's Privacy (COPPA Compliance)

Cobi Paint is designed to be safe for children of all ages:

  • We do not collect personal information from children
  • We do not require registration or login
  • We do not contain advertisements
  • We do not have social features or chat
  • We do not link to external websites

5. Photo Library Access

The app may request access to your photo library to:

  • Import images for coloring
  • Save completed artworks

Photos are processed locally and are never uploaded or shared.

6. Third-Party Services

Cobi Paint does not use any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services.

7. In-App Purchases

The app may offer optional in-app purchases. These transactions are processed securely through Apple's App Store or Google Play Store. We do not have access to your payment information.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date.

9. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at:

Email: cobipaint@gmail.com

Parent's Note: Cobi Paint is designed with your child's safety in mind. The app works completely offline and contains no external links, ads, or social features.
